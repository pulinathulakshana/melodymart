About MelodyMart

Our Story

Founded in 2018 in the heart of Colombo, MelodyMart began as a small family-owned music store with a simple dream: to make quality musical instruments accessible to every Sri Lankan music enthusiast.

What started as a passion project by music lovers has grown into Sri Lanka's most trusted online destination for musical instruments. We've helped thousands of musicians - from curious beginners taking their first steps to seasoned professionals seeking premium instruments.

Today, we're proud to serve customers across all provinces of Sri Lanka, bringing the joy of music to every corner of our beautiful island nation.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform that empowers every Sri Lankan to discover, learn, and excel in their musical journey, fostering a vibrant musical culture across our nation.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional quality instruments, expert guidance, and outstanding service while supporting the growth of Sri Lanka's musical community through education and accessibility.
